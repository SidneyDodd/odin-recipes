Blueberry Pancakes

These blueberry pancakes are an excellent way to start your morning. Fresh blueberries and fluffy pancakes will give you the nutrition and energy you need to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk, plus more if needed
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 stick butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
